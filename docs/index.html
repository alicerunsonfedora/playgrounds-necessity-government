Code your government

The year is 2896 and all existing governments have disappeared off of the face of the earth. It's up to you to create a government that works for the aliens that inhabit the islands. In this Puzzle World-based playground, find out how government plays a role in our lives and why government is necessary.



About this project

This is a collaborative project that I am working on with @DireThunder as a government project to prove that government, no matter how crazy or absurd it is, is still necessary in this day and age.
