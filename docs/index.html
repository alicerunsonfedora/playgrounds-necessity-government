Meet The Government

Play as a new governor for a town in this Minecraft map.
cloud_downloadDownload


group

Hello, governor.

The year is 2896 and all existing governments have disappeared off of the face of the earth. However, a newer, unified government has taken over: The Government. They have appointed you as the new governor for a new town, ready for you to manipulate and control. It's up to you to determine which laws are implemented, how they will be used, and what stays in the town.

Note: This map is built for the cross-platform edition of Minecraft (iOS, Windows 10, Android, Xbox, PS4, etc.).


About this project

This is a collaborative project that I am working on with @DireThunder as a government project to prove that government, no matter how crazy or absurd it is, is still necessary in this day and age.
